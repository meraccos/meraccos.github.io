I am a second year master's student of mechanical engineering at Ulsan National Institute of Science and Technology (UNIST), where I also completed my bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering and computer science & engineering.

Currently, I am working in Autonomous Systems Laboratory (ASL) with the group of Professor Hyondong Oh .Prior to this, I had a chance to work in the Innovative Thermodynamics Laboratory (ITEL) with Professor Jaeseon Lee.

I have also participated in several national and international Physics Olympiads and have been awarded with bronze, one silver, and one gold medals over the span of two years.

I am currently working on the safety and reliability of sequential decision-making algorithms (RL/IL) and their applications in robot autonomy. I have a broad interest in various topics, so please feel free to reach out for potential projects or collaborations!
