Here you can play a simple information theoretic Hangman game. You can choose to play as a guider or as a guesser. If guider, pick a word, press the guess button and guide the agent to find it by pressing on the matching letter locations (skip if there is no match). If guesser, try to guess the word by entering characters one-by-one and pressing the check button.
